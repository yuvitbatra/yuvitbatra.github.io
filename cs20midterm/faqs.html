Frequently Asked Questions

Who can volunteer?

Anyone passionate about teaching and making a difference in children's lives can volunteer.

Do I need teaching experience?

No prior teaching experience is required. We provide guidance and resources to help you.

How can I sign up?

Visit our volunteer page and fill out the application form.

Is there a time commitment?

You can choose flexible time slots that fit your schedule.

Do I get a certificate for volunteering?

Yes! Volunteers who complete a set number of hours will receive a certificate of appreciation.

Is this opportunity online or in-person?

We offer both online and in-person volunteering opportunities based on your preference.

What subjects can I teach?

You can teach subjects like Math, Science, English, and more based on your expertise.

Is there any training before I start?

Yes! We provide training materials and an orientation session to help you get started.
